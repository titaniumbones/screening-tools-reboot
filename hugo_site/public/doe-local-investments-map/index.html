DOE Local Investments Map — Data + Screening Tools

The Local Investment Map for Demonstration and Deployment Projects was published by the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) and last updated in January 2025 to share commitments for local engagement and investment made by projects funded under the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (BIL) and Inflation Reduction Act (IRA). This map identified where DOE’s investments are occurring and additional, high-level snapshots of the local benefits that may be associated with these investments. The projects included were a subset of the selected and awarded BIL and IRA projects. This tool was designed for communities and local leaders to better understand what DOE-supported projects are being developed in their areas, to engage more effectively with the project, and to build long-term project support.
